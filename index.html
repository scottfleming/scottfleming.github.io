Scott Fleming

Ph.D. Student in Biomedical Informatics.

Hi there! I'm a first year Ph.D. student in the Biomedical Informatics (BMI) Graduate Program at Stanford University (housed in the Department of Biomedical Data Science in Stanford's School of Medicine). I am currently rotating with Dr. Nigam Shah. My research focuses on developing clinic-ready tools for sequential decision making under uncertainty, especially in Psychiatry and Mental Health. I am supported by a Stanford Graduate Fellowship. In my spare time, I am an outdoor enthusiast and amateur photographer (the banner above is a photo I took on a recent trip to Yosemite).
